Report of IIIT-H Workshop on Virtual Labs

Organized by CSE Department, Guru Nanak Institute of Technology (GNIT)

The department of CSE, GNIT has organized one day workshop on Virtual Labs by IIIT- Hyderabad Virtual Labs team (http://www.vlabs.ac.in/index.php ) after co-ordinations between Prof. Aniruddha Bhattacharjya, CSE Department, GNIT and Mrs Geeta Bose, Program Manager VLEAD,Virtual Labs, IIIT- Hyderabad on 8th of April , 2015. VIRTUAL LABS is an Initiative of Ministry of Human Resource Development (MHRD) Under the National Mission on Education through ICT. The team from IIIT-Hyd were consisting of five members - 1. Dr.Venkatesh Choppella,Associate Professor,IIIT Hyderabad
2. Mrs. Geeta Bose,Program Manager,Virtual Labs,IIIT Hyderabad 3. Mr. Apurv Tyagi, Project Engineer, VLEAD,Virtual Labs,IIIT Hyderabad 4. Ms. Virupaksha Sadhana,Project Intern, VLEAD,Virtual Labs,IIIT Hyderabad 5. Ms. Modepu Sravanthi, Project Intern, VLEAD, Virtual Labs
All were welcomed by Dr. S. SREENATHA REDDY, Principal, GNIT. They the team meet with Dr. H. S. Saini, MD, GNI .
IIIT Hyderabad The workshop was conducted in two sessions, in the first session Dr.Venkatesh Choppella , explained the need for Virtual Labs and involvements of all IITs in this projects. Then the team showed some demos of virtual labs and in the second session practical sessions using the virtual labs has happened. The second session was held in GNITC lab from 2:00 pm to 4:15 pm ,it was attended by students of CSE and IT departments of GNIT , GNITC. The students who attended this session, done labs experiments using the virtual labs. Students found it very useful for practical learning of subjects. In first session we have 260 students from GNIT and GNITC CSE and IT 3rd Year. In second session we accommodated 75 Students.
Feedback was taken from students who attended, there were very positive feedbacks which included suggestions, improvements required and problems they came across.
Dr. S. SREENATHA REDDY, Principal, GNIT and Dr.M.Narendra Kumar ,Vice- Principal and Dr. Sandeep Singh Rawat, Prof & HOD-CSE & IT and Dr. Aniruddha Bhattacharjya, Professor CSE Department and coordinator of the workshop, GNIT felicitated Dr.Venkatesh Choppella,Associate Professor,IIIT Hyderabad, Mrs. Geeta Bose,Program Manager,Virtual Labs,IIIT Hyderabad , Mr. Apurv Tyagi, Project Engineer, VLEAD,Virtual Labs,IIIT Hyderabad , Ms. Virupaksha Sadhana,Project Intern, VLEAD,Virtual Labs,IIIT Hyderabad, Ms. Modepu Sravanthi, Project Intern, VLEAD, Virtual Labs.

Guru Nanak Institute of Technology:

Ibrahimpatnam Report on Technofest - 2015

Guru Nanak Institutions conducted technical fest- Technofest-2015 on 04-04-2015 for the academic year 2015-16.It was sound platform for Technocrats to exhibit their technical expertise, innovative imaginations coupled with executing capabilities. The fest included paper, poster and project presentations. The fest attracted students from different institution, who actively participated and made the event a glorious success.

Dr. Sandeep Singh Rawat, Head of CSE & IT have encouraged the students to organize the event. Professor B.Sunil have been invited as chief guest. Professor B.Sunil and Professor Imran Qureshi were the jury for the event.

Students have participated in the event very enthusiastically and presented papers in morning session followed by lunch. Afternoon session started with project exhibition and participants have exhibited their working models and student volunteers have ended the event with ought of thanks.

GURU NANAK INSTITUTE OF TECHNOLOGY Dept. of Computer Science Engineering & Information Technology Report on Women in Tech. Webinars
Microsoft Student Associates of Guru Nanak Institute of Technology had conducted two webinar's on Women Empowerment in IT industry on 17th and 31st October 2014. Total girl students participated in two webinar’s were 411.
The session started at 10:00 AM (1 hour session). The participants were given attendee sheets to fill in their information. The event started by the Video of Pratima Amorkar-Director of Marketing and Evangelism Team of Microsoft India.
The webinar was based on the topic “How Technology has changed our lives in past 20 years'' delivered by Rohini Arora-Tech. Evangelist from Microsoft India. It was based on the impact of Technology on our daily lives including Microsoft Technologies like Bing Search, One Drive, Office 365, Skype, Windows 10 and many more.
After the session we (MANISH CHITRE & RAVI TEJA MANTHA) shared our own experiences and motivated girl students for the various advantages of being a Microsoft Student Partner & Microsoft Student Associates and to represent our college at National and International Level.
